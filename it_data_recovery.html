Boxed Paragraph

Data recovery is the process of retrieving lost or inaccessible data from storage devices like hard drives, solid-state drives, USB drives, and memory cards. This may be necessary due to accidental deletion, formatting, corruption, or physical damage to the storage device. Data recovery typically involves scanning the storage device for recoverable data, identifying and extracting the data, and then copying it to a new storage device. This process may require specialized software tools and techniques, and may be performed by individuals or companies that specialize in data recovery services. Successful data recovery depends on a number of factors, including the cause and extent of the data loss, and the effectiveness of the recovery tools and techniques used.
